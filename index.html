Energy that is renewable in Albania

ERA is an organisation that helps renewable energy resources in Albania to be recognised by local and foreign investors. We tend to point out this valuable hidden feature of Albania that not many people are aware of. We promote the widespread adoption and sustainable use of all forms of renewable energy, including biomass, geothermal, hydropower, solar and wind energy, in the pursuit of sustainable development, energy access, energy security and low carbon economic growth and prosperity.
